Shane Moon, PhD

Research Scientist
Facebook Reality Labs

I'm Shane (Seungwhan) Moon, and I am a Research Scientist at Facebook Reality Labs working on Conversational AI and Multimodal Learning. Before that, I completed my PhD at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) where I worked with Prof. Jaime Carbonell on Proactive Transfer Learning and Multimodal Learning with various applications in NLP and CV.

Some of my non-academic pursuits include photography, music, cycling, etc.

Email: me at shanemoon dot com
